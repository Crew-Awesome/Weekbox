# Code of Conduct

WeekBox is a community project made by Crew Awesome. Treat contributors and users with respect.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, patient, and considerate.
- Focus criticism on development and code, not developers.
- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels, and opinions.
- Report suspected security or privacy problems in the app responsibly.

## Unacceptable behavior

Bringing unrelated drama into the project, Harassment, threats, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, or sharing another person's private information without permission are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or restrict participation when needed to protect the community. Report conduct concerns to a maintainer through the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Crew-Awesome/Weekbox).
